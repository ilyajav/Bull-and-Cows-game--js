Welcome to Bulls and Cows game!

Rules:

At the beginning of the game, the machine thinks out 4 different random numbers.
The aim of the game is to guess the numbers that the machine has guessed.
The bull means that in the given sequence there is a number that is in the hidden sequence and is in the correct position.
A cow indicates that a number was given, but it is in the wrong position. Your numbers have to different.
Have a nice game!
